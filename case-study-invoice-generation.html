Automating Invoice Generation

How AutomataX developed a GUI-based invoice generator to streamline invoice creation, PDF generation, and email distribution, saving time and reducing errors.

Client Overview

A mid-sized service provider managing multiple service centers (SCs) relied on manual processes to generate invoices from Excel data. This involved grouping data, calculating amounts, creating PDF invoices, and emailing them to SCs, leading to significant time consumption, errors, and inefficiencies.

Challenges

  • Manual data aggregation: Grouping Excel data by SC and claim status required hours of manual work.
  • Error-prone calculations: Manual GST calculations and amount conversions led to frequent discrepancies.
  • Time-consuming PDF creation: Manually formatting invoices in Excel or Word was labor-intensive and inconsistent.
  • Email distribution: Composing emails and attaching files for each SC was repetitive and prone to errors.
  • Lack of consolidation: Summarizing invoice details across SCs for reporting purposes was a tedious manual task.

Our Solution

We developed a Python-based desktop application, BillCraft, using Python-based libraries for a modern GUI, to fully automate the invoice generation process:

  • Automated data processing: Scripted logic to group Excel data by SC and claim status, applying configured price rates for accurate calculations.
  • Dynamic PDF generation: Used ReportLab to create professional PDF invoices with consistent formatting, including GST summaries and amount in words.
  • Automated email sending: Integrated SMTP to send invoices and raw data files to SCs, with CCs to relevant stakeholders.
  • Consolidated reporting: Generated a summary Excel file for all invoices, streamlining financial reporting.
  • User-friendly GUI: Provided an intuitive interface for configuration, logging, and process initiation, with modern styling and real-time feedback.

Implementation Process

Phase 1: Analysis & Design

Analyzed raw data structure, identified key requirements (e.g., GST logic, email automation), and designed a user-friendly GUI with configuration options.

Phase 2: Development

Built the application with PySide6 for the GUI, Pandas for data processing, ReportLab for PDF generation, and smtplib for email automation, ensuring robust error handling.

Phase 3: Testing & Deployment

Tested with sample data, refined GST calculations and email functionality, deployed the application with a user guide, and trained staff for seamless adoption.
