Transforming Industrial Safety Management

How AutomataX automated Permit to Work (PTW) processes to save time, reduce errors, and enhance workplace safety compliance.

Overview

A major tech driven company with operations across multiple sites was struggling with their manual Permit to Work (PTW) process. With over 500 PTW forms processed monthly across their facilities, the manual handling was creating bottlenecks, inconsistencies, and safety risks.

AutomataX partnered with the client to implement a comprehensive RPA solution that digitized and automated the entire PTW workflow, resulting in significant time savings, error reduction, and enhanced safety compliance.

Challenges

  • Time-consuming process with each PTW form requiring approximately 45 minutes to complete manually
  • High error rates of 15% due to manual data entry, creating potential safety hazards
  • Documentation issues with tracking and storing physical forms resulting in missing paperwork
  • Approval delays from multi-tiered approval processes creating workflow bottlenecks
  • Limited visibility for management into ongoing work permits and their status

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive RPA solution to digitize and automate the entire PTW workflow:

  • Digital form creation with a user-friendly interface for PTW form submission
  • Automated validation to ensure all required fields are completed correctly
  • Workflow automation with automated routing for multi-level approvals and notification systems
  • Integration capabilities connecting the PTW system with existing maintenance management software
  • Real-time dashboard showing PTW status and key metrics for management oversight
  • Secure digital repository for all PTW documents with powerful search capabilities

Implementation Process

Phase 1: Assessment & Design

Conducted in-depth analysis of existing PTW processes, mapped current workflows, identified bottlenecks, and created detailed requirements specifications.

Phase 2: Development

Built the digital PTW form interface, developed the RPA bots for form processing, created the approval workflow engine, and integrated with existing systems.

Phase 3: Testing & Deployment

Performed rigorous testing, conducted user acceptance testing with key stakeholders, provided comprehensive training, and implemented a phased rollout.

By implementing our RPA solution for PTW automation, the client achieved significant operational efficiencies, cost savings, and safety improvements.
