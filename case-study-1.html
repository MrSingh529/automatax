Transforming Industrial Safety

How AutomataX automated Permit to Work (PTW) processes to save time, reduce errors, and enhance workplace safety compliance.

Overview

A tech-driven company with operations across multiple sites faced challenges with their manual Permit to Work (PTW) process. Processing over 500 PTW forms monthly, the manual system caused bottlenecks, inconsistencies, and safety hazards. AutomataX implemented an RPA solution to digitize and automate the PTW workflow, delivering significant time savings, error reduction, and enhanced safety compliance.

Challenges

  • Each PTW form took ~45 minutes to complete, slowing operations.
  • 15% error rate from manual data entry posed safety risks.
  • Tracking and storing physical forms led to missing documentation.
  • Multi-tiered approvals caused delays and bottlenecks.
  • Limited management visibility into PTW statuses.

Our Solution

We deployed a comprehensive RPA solution to streamline the PTW process:

  • User-friendly digital form interface for PTW submissions.
  • Automated validation to ensure accurate form completion.
  • Automated routing for multi-level approvals with notifications.
  • Integration with existing maintenance management systems.
  • Real-time dashboard for PTW status and metrics.
  • Secure digital repository with powerful search capabilities.

Implementation Process

Phase 1: Assessment & Design

Analyzed existing PTW processes, mapped workflows, and defined requirements.

Phase 2: Development

Developed digital PTW forms, RPA bots, approval workflows, and system integrations.

Phase 3: Testing & Deployment

Conducted testing, user acceptance, training, and phased rollout.
