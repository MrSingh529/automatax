Industrial Safety, Transformed

See how AutomataX redefined safety management with elite, AI-powered PTW automation—delivering rapid efficiency gains, flawless compliance, and a new gold standard for operational excellence.

Project Snapshot

This forward-thinking client was ready to leave outdated, manual PTW headaches behind. With hundreds of forms choking productivity and risking compliance, AutomataX delivered a high-impact, automated solution—streamlining workflows, eliminating errors, and unlocking full visibility for safer, smarter operations.

Key Challenges

  • Manual PTW forms averaged 45+ minutes each—slowing productivity.
  • Costly 15% error rate threatened compliance and safety.
  • Paper-based storage meant lost docs and audit risk.
  • Complex approvals stalled projects with frustrating delays.
  • Zero real-time insight for management oversight or action.

AutomataX in Action

Our RPA experts unleashed automation across the PTW lifecycle:

  • Sleek, intuitive digital forms for instant, on-site PTW requests.
  • Built-in smart validation to eliminate errors at the source.
  • Lightning-fast, multi-level approvals—fully automated with alerts.
  • Seamless integrations with the client’s core maintenance systems.
  • Live dashboards for full status transparency and instant decision-making.
  • Secure digital archiving—no lost forms, no compliance gaps, ever.

Implementation Process

Phase 1: Assessment & Design

AutomataX specialists mapped every workflow, spotlighting pain points and customizing a next-gen solution for flawless fit.

Phase 2: Development

Developed digital PTW forms, RPA bots, approval workflows, and system integrations.

Phase 3: Testing & Deployment

Conducted testing, user acceptance, training, and phased rollout.
